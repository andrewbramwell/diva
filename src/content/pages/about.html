Our Story

About Diva Thai Massage & Beauty

Welcome to Diva Thai Massage & Beauty, where traditional Thai healing meets modern wellness in the heart of Cardiff. Founded by Mai, our establishment brings authentic Thai massage techniques and contemporary beauty treatments together under one roof. Originally from the historic city of Sukhothai, Thailand, Mai brings her expertise and deep-rooted understanding of traditional Thai massage to Cardiff.

With a passionate dedication to massage therapy, Mai established Diva Thai Massage & Beauty in Cardiff after gaining valuable experience at Thai Harmony in London. Her commitment to excellence is reflected in her impressive credentials, including certification from The Union of Thai Traditional Medicine Society and comprehensive training in various therapeutic techniques through Vicki Clarke Cosmetology.

At Diva Thai Massage & Beauty, we pride ourselves on our holistic approach to wellness and beauty. Our services range from traditional Thai massage and deep tissue treatments to contemporary beauty services like facial therapy and body waxing. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to customer care and our focus on injury treatment and recovery.

My passion lies in helping people recover and feel their best. Every client who walks through our door receives not just a treatment, but a caring, personalised experience focused on their wellbeing. Mai Owner
